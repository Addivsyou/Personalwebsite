DWJ30 Closing Price Forecast

I know that predicting the market is impossible, but I wanted to give it a try. This is my first attempt: less than a week of work and already positive results.

The artificial intelligence based on the XGBoost algorithm has been trained with daily price and volume datasets starting from January 1, 2020. The results can be viewed on a Power BI dashboard. As expected, many predictions turned out to be inaccurate. However, some tickers like BRK-B, PG, ABT, MCD, and KO surprised us with an accuracy exceeding 99%. This success has motivated me to delve deeper into the project and aim for something revolutionary. With the help of a trader friend, we will combine trading strategies with the XGBoost and LSTM algorithms, already integrated into version 2.
